1.	Melt 6 tablespoons butter in heavy extra-large pot over medium-high heat.
2.	Add onions; sprinkle with salt and pepper and sauté until onions begin to soften and turn golden.
3.	Transfer 1 1/2 cups caramelized onions to small bowl and reserve for garnish.
4.	Add 8 cups broth to remaining onions in pot and bring to boil. Reduce heat and simmer 25 minutes to blend flavors.
5.	Cool slightly. Working in batches, puree soup in blender until smooth. Return to same pot and add more broth by 1/4 cupfuls to thin soup to desired consistency.
6.	Season soup to taste with salt and pepper. Cool soup slightly. 
7.	Cook remaining 4 tablespoons butter in small saucepan over medium heat until golden brown, stirring occasionally, 3 to 4 minutes.
8.	Add sage leaves and cook until slightly crisp, 30 seconds to 1 minute. Transfer leaves to small plate; reserve brown butter in saucepan.
9.	Stir 1 1/2 tablespoons vinegar into reserved caramelized onions, adding more vinegar by 1/2 teaspoonfuls, if desired.
